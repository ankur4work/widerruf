## Full description

**Be compliant with EU Directive 2023/2673 (Art. 11a) without breaking your store.**

Widerruf adds a clearly-labelled withdrawal button (footer link or floating) via an
App Embed block, plus a dedicated, server-rendered withdrawal page at
`/apps/withdrawal`. Nothing in your theme is modified and no page is replaced.

### How it works with your customers' data
- **Customers submit a withdrawal request** with their **name, email address**, order
  number, the item/service, and an optional reason. We use this to create the legally
  required withdrawal record and confirm receipt.
- **We email the customer on your behalf** — an immediate durable-medium confirmation,
  and, when you accept or reject the request, a decision email you can preview and edit
  before it is sent.
- **Order matching (uses order access):** the form can verify that the order number and
  email match a real order, and links each request to the correct Shopify order.

### Free — genuinely
- Live withdrawal button + form (no "test mode")
- Automatic confirmation email with exact timestamp
- Merchant dashboard with full audit trail
- Editable form text and decision-email templates
- Single-request PDF evidence pack
- All 24 EU languages

### Pro
- **Auto-cancel unfulfilled orders on withdrawal** (with restock)
- **Refund automation** — issue a full refund when you accept a withdrawal
- **Send emails from your own domain**
- **PDF evidence packs + bulk CSV/PDF export**
- **Analytics & withdrawal reasons**
- Remove branding + priority support

### Data & privacy
Widerruf processes only the data needed to record, confirm, and act on a withdrawal
request. It never sells data or uses it for marketing. See our Privacy Policy:
`https://[APP_DOMAIN]/privacy`.

> This app is a software tool to help you comply with EU Directive 2023/2673. It is not
> legal advice.

## Why the app needs each permission (for the reviewer / PCD form)
- **read_orders + Email field** — validate that the order number + email match a real
  order and link the request to it.
- **write_orders (Store management)** — Pro automation cancels the unfulfilled order and
  issues the refund when you process a withdrawal.
- **Name + Email fields (App functionality + Customer service)** — the withdrawal record
  legally requires the customer's name; email is used to confirm receipt and send your
  accept/reject decision.
